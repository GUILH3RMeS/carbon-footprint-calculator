Projects we support monthly

Amazon Forest healing

We donate every month to reforestation of Amazon Forest, the biggest tropical forest in the world. With the contributions, the NGOs plant, protect and monitor the new trees.

Preservation of endangered species

We're loosing, every day, animals that are fundamental to the global ecossystem. We need to proctect them from the massive extinction. Help us to support sanctuaries and save animals.

Cleaning the oceans

Without marine life, there's no life on Earth. More than 50% of our oxygen is produced under water. If we don't save the oceans, it will be impossible to save ourselves.

Protect indigenous communities

Indigenous people are the front line of forest defense around the globe. They need our help to fight against companies that aim to burn our natural resources down in order to get profit.

Solar energy production

There's no society without eletricity. There's no futre without clean energy. We support Remote Energy, a NGO that prepare professionals to install and develop solar energy massively.

Climate change news

To solve a problem, we need to talk about it. The Hourglass project was created to make climate change part of the conversation and give voice to people and iniciatives that fight for planet healing.
