What is a carbon footprint calculator?

The carbon foot print calculator is a tool that uses data on consumption behavior NEWS CLIMATE CHANGE ABOUT US Donate The carbon footprint calculator is a tool that uses data on consumption behavior and lifestyle to understand what your sources of carbon emissions are.

The idea is that you have a dimension, depending on your habits, on the amount of carbon you emit into the atmosphere through your choices in food, transportation and other aspects of everyday life.

How is the carbon footprint calculated?

As carbon is one of the gasses of greatest concern when it comes to climate change, there are studies available with the average or exact measurement of each activity we usually perform in our day, such as taking the car to go to work, for example.

In this way, an individual's carbon footprint is calculated based on all of their choices and the intensity of this emission, in order to understand what their carbon footprint is projected during the year, the decade or throughout their lifetime.

Does carbon affect climate change?

Carbon greatly affects climate change. It is one of the main greenhouse gasses, along with methane, another major concern for scientists.

Greenhouse gasses such as carbon dioxide (CO2) have the power to trap radiation from the sun. So the more gasses like this we have in the atmosphere, the hotter it tends to get (hence the name greenhouse effect).

If we could achieve total zero carbon emissions, we could simply halt the rapid advance of global warming. As this would be impossible, it is necessary to zero the carbon footprint in sectors where we have a choice, such as the generation of electricity, fuel or the generation of waste
